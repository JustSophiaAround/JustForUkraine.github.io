20220315 'Choose dignity': Zelenskiy urges Russian troops to surrender BY THE GUARDIANS

20220308 「選擇擁有尊嚴」：烏總統呼籲俄羅斯軍隊盡快投降

新聞原址

英文原文

Ukraine president Volodymyr Zelenskiy has his latest address to urge Russian troops to choose giving up arms over the “shame” of continuing with the war.

Speaking in Russian, Zelenskiy said: “On behalf of the Ukrainian people, we give you a chance to live. If you surrender to our forces, we will treat you as humans have to be treated: with dignity. The way you have not been treated in your army. And the way your army doesn’t treat our people. Choose.”

Ukrainian president Volodymyr Zelenskiy. Photograph: Telegram

He also said the war had become a “nightmare” for the Russians,and claimed that their troops were fleeing the battlefield and leaving arms and equipment for the Ukrainians.

Here is the first part of his speech. It’s a long address so I’ll post the rest in two more chunks.

Free people of the free country, the 19th day of our resistance has come to end. Another hard day that made us closer to the victory and peace for Ukraine. As before, the enemy is perplexed. They didn’t expect such resistance. They believed their propaganda that lied about us for decades.

They still can’t recover but have begun realizing that they won’t achieve anything by war. Their soldiers know it. Their officers understand it. They flee from the battlefield, leaving behind their vehicles and equipment. We take the trophies and use them for defence of Ukraine. Russian forces have de-facto become a supplier of equipment for our army. They couldn’t imagine it in a nightmare.

[In Russian] I want to tell Russian soldiers, those who have already entered our lands and those who are only being sent to fight against us. Russian conscripts, please, listen to me attentively. Russian officers, you have already understood everything. You won’t be able to take anything from Ukraine. You will take lives – you are many – but yours will be taken too.

What are you dying for? I know, you want to survive. We hear in your intercepted calls what you really think of this war, of this shame, and of your state. Your conversations with each other, your calls home to your families, we hear everything. We make conclusions. We know who you are. That’s why I offer you a choice: On behalf of the Ukrainian people, we give you a chance to live. If you surrender to our forces, we will treat you as humans have to be treated: with dignity. The way you have not been treated in your army. And the way your army doesn’t treat our people. Choose.

烏克蘭總統弗拉基米爾·澤連斯基在最新的談話中，呼籲俄羅斯軍隊選擇丟下武器，減少持續戰爭的恥辱

澤倫斯基以俄文表示，「我謹代表烏克蘭人給予你們活下來的機會。如果你向我們的軍隊投降，我們會對待你們如同對待人類該有的待遇，也就是有尊嚴的對待。」 這是你們在軍隊裡沒有的待遇。這也是你們沒有正確對待我們人民的的方式。做出選擇吧。」

他還說，這場戰爭對於俄羅斯人來說，已經變成一個夢魘，並宣稱他們的軍隊在戰場上逃走，並給烏克蘭人留下裝備和武器。

這是他演講的第一部分，並且這是一篇十分長的講稿，所以我會把剩下的分成兩部分放上來(原新聞網)。

自由的國家的自由的人民，我們奮力抵抗的第十九天已經結束。又是艱難的一天，讓烏克蘭更加接近勝利與和平。就如同之前，我們的敵人迷茫著。 他們沒有預期這個程度的抵抗。他們相信一直以來的政治宣傳，相信那些關於我們虛假而欺騙性質的，已經進行數十年的傳聞。

他們尚未從震驚中恢復，但也開始瞭解他們並不會在戰爭中達到任何目的。他們的士兵知道這件事。 他們的士官們知道這件事。他們從戰場上逃離，丟下他們的駕駛工具和裝備。我們拿走我們的戰利品，並將其用來自衛烏克蘭。 俄羅斯的軍隊已經成為，即使他們不承認，我們軍隊的裝備提供者。他們在這麼一個噩夢中簡直無法想像。

[俄文] 我要告訴俄羅斯士兵們，那些已經踏上我們的土地的和被送來要和我們打仗的人，俄羅斯的徵召兵，請注意的聽我說。 俄羅斯的軍官們，你們早已知曉一切。你們不會有機會在烏克蘭拿走任何東西。你會拿走生命－你們有很多人－但你們的生命也會被拿走。

你們是為何而死？我知道，你們想要存活。我們聽到你們被我們截獲的電話，聽到你們對於這場戰爭真正的想法，對於這個恥辱和你的國家真正的想法。

你們和彼此的對話，你們打回家給家人的電話，我們已聽到所有。我們做出結論。我們知道你們是誰。 那就是我給予你們一個選擇的原因：我代表烏克蘭人民，給你們一個活下去的機會。 如果你向我們的軍隊投誠，我們會對待你們就向人類該如何被對待的一樣：讓你們有尊嚴地被對待。這是你們在軍隊裡沒有的待遇。這也是你們沒有正確對待我們人民的的方式。做出選擇吧。



20220308

Mr. Zerenski announced that he will stay until the winning of the war by giving a nine minute speech in his office

新聞原址

中文原文

俄羅斯入侵烏克蘭以來，烏克蘭總統澤倫斯基首度重返自己的辦公室。澤倫斯基錄製影片表示，他不會躲，不怕任何人，將留下直到贏得這場戰爭。（戚海倫報導）

烏克蘭總統澤倫斯基當地周一晚上重返自己在基輔的辦公室。他說，我住在基輔，在我的辦公室，我沒有躲藏，也不怕任何人。

澤倫斯基坐在總統府辦公桌前，發表了9分鐘演講，他表示，烏克蘭軍隊堅守陣地。星期一是艱難的一天，而如今國內有戰爭，所以每天都是星期一，我們持續在工作。每個人都在自己應該在的地方，團隊跟我在一起，軍人、醫生、救援人員、運輸人員、外交官、記者，我們都在打仗，我們一定會取得勝利，透過武力和軍隊、透過言語和外交手段、也透過精神力量。

澤倫斯基也對民眾喊話，昨天、今天和明天，對抗侵略者的烏克蘭男女，每一位都是英雄。他也說，在烏克蘭南部，對俄羅斯來說宛如噩夢，因為他們忘記了，烏克蘭人不怕坦克和機關槍。他也說，將重建一切，讓被侵略者摧毀的城市，比俄羅斯任何城市都好。烏克蘭將堅持談判，直到找到方法告訴人民，這就是我們實現和平的方式。


英文翻譯

Since the Russians invaded Ukraine, the President of Ukraine returned to his office for the first time. He recorded a video to show his determination of not hiding nor being afraid of anyone, and he will stay until the victory of Ukraine.

The President of Ukraine Mr. Zerenski returned to his office in Kyiv on Monday night local time. "I live in Kyiv, and I'm at my office, not hiding, nor am I afraid of anyone." says the President.

A nine-minute speech was made before the desk of the Office of the President by Mr. Zerenski. He states that the Force of Ukraine hold fast to their position. "Mondays are difficult, and now war is happening in Ukraine. Therefore, it's Monday everyday, and we continue to work hard. Everyone is at their place, the team is also with me. Soldiers, doctors, the rescue team, materials handlers, diplomats, reporters, we are all fighting, and we'll definitely achieve victory. Through arms and troops， through words and diplomatic moves, and also through strengths of spirituality."

The president also calls out to the people. "Yesterday, today, and tomorrow, every man and woman who stands up to invadors is a hero." he says. "At the South of Ukraine, it is like a nightmare to Russia, before they have forgotten the fact that Ukrainians aren't afraid of tanks and machine guns." He also indicates that they will rebuild everything, including the cities that were destroyed by the invadors, and make them better than any city of Russia.

"Ukraine will insist on negotiating, until we find out a solution and tell it to the people. This is how we realize peace."
